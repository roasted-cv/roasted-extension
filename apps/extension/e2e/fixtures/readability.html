Product Designer

Northwind is a small but fast-growing company building tools for independent makers, and we are looking for a Product Designer who can own the end-to-end experience of our flagship product. You will partner closely with engineering and product to turn ambiguous problems into clear, usable interfaces that customers love.

In this role you will lead design for major initiatives, from early discovery and research through high-fidelity execution and hand-off. You will maintain and evolve our design system, run usability studies with real customers, and shape the overall visual language of the product as it matures over the coming years.

We expect several years of experience designing and shipping digital products, a strong portfolio that demonstrates craft and systems thinking, and the ability to communicate decisions clearly to technical and non-technical stakeholders alike. Experience with motion design or front-end code is a welcome bonus.
